Interesting Quiz for teenagers to gain new knowledges through informal education

At our meetings, where through informal learning, young people learned a lot of interesting information about politics, democracy, and elections to the European Parliament. With each meeting, we saw how young people’s interest in politics grew. At our meetings there were speakers who were specialists in the field of Politics and International Relations, such as members of the European Parliament, US Congress Woman, deputies of the Youth Seimas of Latvia, advisors of the United Nations, students of Columbia University and the University of Maine.

We saw that with each meeting we were moving towards our goal, we saw how young people participate in discussions and ask interesting questions. At each meeting of our Youth Political Society “YPS” we organised amusing Quiz. We saw how interesting it was for teenagers to gain new knowledge through informal education. It's always exciting!

And we created Quiz on our website. That is everyone will be able to feel themselves in the world of politics! Learn a lot of new things and take part in the Quiz at any time convenient for you!
