Future Heroes Latvia

Future Heroes is a growth mindset-driven leadership & entrepreneurship program for girls aged 14-17 to unlock their potential and create positive impact in the world.

We had a great opportunity to start our own project, to meet inspirational people, to participate in public speaking, to meet new friends!

We received the best knowledges about leadership skills and businesses. Future heroes program really helps to become your own hero and become better version of yourself!
