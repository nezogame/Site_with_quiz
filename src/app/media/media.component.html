We are in Media

Young Political Society was invited to Radio Pieci.Iv.

We talked about our great project the Future Heroes of Latvia.

We discussed the main goals of our organisation "YPS":

  • to increase the interest of young people in politics;
  • strengthen the democratic environment in Latvia
  • involve young people to actively participate in the future of their country
  • increase the level of patriotism to Latvia of young people
  • we have been pro-voting since the age of 16!

"Youth's voice in politics - force for change!"

Our aim is to create a political movement that will encourage the government of Latvia to give teenagers a voice to participate in decision-making on a governmental level.
